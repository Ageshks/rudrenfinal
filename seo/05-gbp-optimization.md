## Google Reviews Strategy

**Review Generation Tactics:**
1. Send review link via WhatsApp immediately after service completion
2. Create a QR code linking to GBP review page — place on invoices and delivery receipts
3. Follow up with a phone call 48 hours after delivery to confirm satisfaction, then request review
4. Offer a small incentive (e.g., 5% discount on next order) for leaving a review

**Review Response Protocol:**
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours
- Positive reviews: Thank the customer, mention specific service/product they used
- Negative reviews: Apologize, offer to resolve offline, then follow up publicly

**Sample Positive Response:**
> "Thank you for your kind words, [Name]! We're glad our [service/product] met your expectations. It was a pleasure serving your team at [Company Name]. Looking forward to continuing our partnership!"

**Sample Negative Response:**
> "Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We apologize for the inconvenience. Please contact us at +91 96070 24997 so we can address your concerns immediately. We value your business and want to make this right."
