### Linking Rules

1. Every page links back to its parent pillar page
2. Every pillar page links to its cluster pages
3. Blog posts link to relevant service/product pages
4. Location pages link to services pages
5. Contact page linked from ALL pages (CTA)
6. Use descriptive anchor text with keywords (not "click here")
